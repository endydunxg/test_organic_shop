# 🌿 Organic Shop – Design System

## 1. Overview
This design system defines the UI/UX principles, components, and layout structure for an Organic Shop e-commerce web application.

The goal is to create a clean, natural, and trustworthy shopping experience that emphasizes freshness, sustainability, and simplicity.

---

## 2. Design Principles

- 🌿 Natural & Organic: Use green tones, earthy colors, and soft UI
- 🧘 Minimal & Clean: Avoid clutter, focus on product visibility
- 🤝 Trustworthy: Highlight reviews, certifications, and product origin
- ⚡ Fast & Simple: Easy navigation and quick checkout

---

Neutral:

## 4. Typography

Font Family:
- Primary: "Poppins", sans-serif
- Secondary: "Inter", sans-serif

Heading:
- Bold, clean, slightly rounded

Body:
- Medium weight, easy to read

## 7. UI Style

- Rounded corners (8px – 16px)
- Soft shadows
- Large images for products
- Plenty of white space

---

## 8. Icons

- Use simple line icons
- Style: minimal, clean
- Examples:
  - Cart
  - User
  - Heart (wishlist)

---

## 9. Animations

- Smooth hover effects
- Button hover: slightly darker green
- Card hover: scale up slightly

---

## 10. Responsive Design

- Mobile-first approach
- Breakpoints:
  - Mobile: < 768px
  - Tablet: 768px – 1024px
  - Desktop: > 1024px

---

## 11. Special Features

- 🌱 “100% Organic” badge on products
- ⭐ Customer reviews and ratings
- 📍 Product origin information
- 🕒 Delivery estimate

## 12. UX Flow

2. Browses products
3. Views product details
4. Adds to cart
